Questions

Fair questions, straight answers.

Is this gambling?
No. TiltProof is play-money only — the chips have no cash value, can’t be bought, and can’t be cashed out. It’s a training app, like a chess app for poker — nothing of value to win or lose.
Will this make me money?
We make no money promises — anyone who does is selling something. What TiltProof does is measurable: it finds your leaks, prices them, and trains them out. Your TiltScore shows whether your decisions are actually getting better.
I’ve never played poker. Is this for me?
Yes — the 2-minute skill check places you, and the learning path starts at “what beats what.” The coach explains everything in plain English, and nobody’s watching you learn.
